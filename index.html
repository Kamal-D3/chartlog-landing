FAQ

Common questions

Does the AI really adapt to my strategy?+
Yes. Every time you log a trade, you specify the indicator or strategy you used (Ichimoku, SMC, RSI, Price Action, etc.). The AI reads this and adapts its entire analysis to your approach.
What markets and instruments are supported?+
All of them. Indices (DAX, S&P500, Nasdaq, CAC40, FTSE...), individual stocks, Forex, Crypto, Commodities. If you can trade it, you can journal it.
Is my data safe?+
Your data is stored on Supabase with row-level security — only you can access your trades. We never share or sell your data. You can export everything in JSON at any time.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes, cancel anytime with one click. No questions asked. Your data stays accessible on the Free plan even after downgrading.
What AI powers the coach?+
Chartlog uses Claude by Anthropic — one of the most capable AI models available today. The same technology used by Fortune 500 companies, adapted specifically for trading analysis and coaching.
